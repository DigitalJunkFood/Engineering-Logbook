# Session 002

**Date:** July 3, 2026

## Objective

Begin construction of the greenhouse enclosure and improve hardware reliability.

## Mechanical Progress

Collected cardstock and additional materials to begin construction of the greenhouse frame and modular plant trays.

Measured the enclosure dimensions to ensure the greenhouse box would properly fit over the planting area while remaining structurally supported.

## Hardware Failure

Repeated handling caused the wires attached to the DC motor to break.

The motor was unsupported during testing, placing excessive strain on the wire connections.

## Planned Improvement

Purchase a helping-hands workstation or PCB vise to support components while soldering and testing.

Expected benefits include:

- Reduced wire fatigue
- Easier soldering
- Improved reliability
- Better repeatability during testing

## Lessons Learned

Mechanical support is just as important as electrical design.
