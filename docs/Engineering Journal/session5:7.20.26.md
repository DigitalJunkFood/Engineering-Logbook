## PCB Repair

Experienced difficulty removing solder from industrial circuit boards.

Current setup:

- Iron temperature: 668°F

Changes tested:

- Standard conical tip
- Larger chisel tip

The wider tip transferred heat more effectively, but larger joints still required significant heating before melting.

Observed that solder cooled rapidly, making removal with a solder sucker difficult.

Future testing will compare solder wick against the pump for smaller joints.
